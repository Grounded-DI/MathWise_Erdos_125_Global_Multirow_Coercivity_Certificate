SCOPE AND LIMITATIONS

Certified statement
For the uniquely certified target c*, on the fixed interval [0,H2],

  1/2 I <= W2(c*) <= 2 I,

in the Loewner order.

Fixed data
- H2 = 1260233493541651302622586638887192915605138797326
- target shifts: (1,0), (3,0), (0,1), (-1,1), (-3,1)
- transition: 3^96/4^76 -> 3^101/4^80
- source operator: W1 = I - (127/768) D_{s1,3}

Uniqueness
The optimal target c* is unique and the optimal target face has dimension zero.
The auxiliary Schur completions are not unique. Certified local dimensions are
91, 105, and 225, for total local auxiliary-completion dimension 421.

Limitations
No assertion is made for other intervals, target shifts, coefficient vectors,
source operators, transition architectures, or residual problems.
The release contains no digital signature and claims only SHA-256 integrity.
